Hi, I am **Geo Ahn**, an M.S. graduate in Computer Science &amp; Engineering at [Kyung Hee University](https://www.khu.ac.kr/eng/),
where I was advised by [Prof. Jinwoo Choi](https://sites.google.com/site/jchoivision/) at the
[KHU Vision and Learning Lab](https://sites.google.com/site/jchoivision/).
I received my B.S. in Software Convergence (Data Science track) from the same university in 2024.

My research interests lie in **video understanding**, **multimodal large language models (MLLMs)**, and **action recognition**.
I am particularly interested in building models that generalize beyond spurious shortcuts — e.g., object-, scene-, or background-driven biases —
toward holistic, compositional reasoning over actions in videos.

Most recently, I was a research intern at **NAVER Cloud (Video Team)**, working on zero-shot compositional action recognition with vision-language models.
